This assignment focused on responsive design, that is getting all the web content to be adaptable for different viewports. Changing resolution will make the content adapt to display 4 x 2 rows on larger screens, 2 x 4 on the smaller screens and 1 x 8 on the smallest screens. Each card also has a fade-in button that does not do anything.
